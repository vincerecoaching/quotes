# Kirk Neal

> Kirk Neal is a business coach for trade business owners across Australia and New Zealand. With 9 years experience and 3,000+ clients coached through Vincere Coaching & Consulting, Kirk helps trade business owners build profitable, systemised businesses. Average client result: an extra $60,000 per month in revenue within 12 months.

Kirk does not use a one-size-fits-all program. Every trade business is different — different trade, different market, different owner. His approach is to find the right strategy for each individual client.

## Programs

- [Tradie Booster](https://kirkneal.com/coaching/): Entry-level coaching membership for trade business owners building their foundations — pricing, systems, team management.
- [Elite Coaching](https://kirkneal.com/coaching/): 1:1 coaching for established trade businesses ready to scale and step back from the day-to-day.
- [Executive Coaching](https://kirkneal.com/coaching/): Bespoke 1:1 coaching for trade business owners ready to lead at the highest level.

## Workshops

- [Trade Business Workshops](https://kirkneal.com/workshops/): Practical team workshops delivered across Australia.

## Speaking
