# Tradie Booster — Landing Page Design Spec
## (For Tekmatix Page Builder)

---

## Brand Colours

| Role | Hex |
|---|---|
| Primary Orange | `#F47920` |
| Background Black | `#0D0D0D` |
| White | `#FFFFFF` |
| Dark Charcoal (body text) | `#1A1A1A` |

**Rule:** Orange + Black = hero sections, CTAs, price callouts. White = content/pain point sections.

---

## Typography

- **Headlines:** Montserrat ExtraBold (800–900 weight) — mix black + orange words within same headline
- **Body:** Montserrat Regular or Open Sans Regular
- **Price callout:** Extra-large bold numerals, orange
- **Section labels:** Small caps, orange, with short orange horizontal rule beneath

---

## Page Layout — Section by Section

### 1. HEADER / NAV
- Black background
- Vincere logo top-left (white version)
- No nav links — this is a conversion page, no exits

---

### 2. HERO SECTION
- **Background:** Black, full-width
- **Top label:** "TRADE BUSINESS GROWTH" — small caps, orange, with orange rule below
- **Headline (large):** Mix of white + orange words
  - e.g. "Build a **Profitable**, **Systemised** Trade Business"
- **Subheadline:** White, regular weight — use Perth plumbers hook
- **Price badge:** Orange rounded rectangle, top-right corner — "ONLY $97/month"
- **Hero image:** Dark/moody tradie photo OR tradie silhouette (hammer raised) on orange panel
- **CTA button:** Orange fill, white bold text, rounded — "Join Tradie Booster — $97/month →"
- **Trust line:** White small text — "No lock-in. Cancel anytime. 30-day money-back guarantee."

---

### 3. VSL SECTION
- **Background:** Near-black `#0D0D0D`
- Video embed centred, max-width ~800px
- Caption below: orange italic text — *"Watch this before you do anything else."*
- CTA button repeated below video

---

### 4. PAIN POINTS SECTION — "Sound Familiar?"
- **Background:** White
- **Headline:** "Sound Familiar?" — "Sound" in black, "Familiar?" in orange, orange underline accent
- **Checklist:** Orange circle-tick icons, dark charcoal text
  - Flat out but still not making what you should
  - Struggling to win consistent work
  - Discounting to secure jobs
  - Constantly putting out fires
  - Business relies too heavily on you
  - Lying awake wondering if you're making money or just staying busy
- **Orange callout box** (rounded rectangle, orange background, white text):
  - "And here's what really stings — you're one of the hardest workers around. But your body has a ceiling. You can't keep doing this pace forever."

---

### 5. CREDIBILITY SECTION
- **Background:** Black
- **Kirk headshot:** Circular or square, orange paint-splatter graphic behind it (brand-consistent)
- **Name:** Orange bold
- **Title:** "Trade Business Coach" — orange
- **Body:** White regular weight
  - "20+ years in the electrical industry. 9 years coaching. 3,000+ trade business owners across Australia and New Zealand."
  - "Every framework inside Tradie Booster was built for trade businesses — not borrowed from somewhere else and repackaged."
- **Contact details** (optional): Orange icon circles — phone, email, website

---

### 6. WHAT YOU GET SECTION
- **Background:** White
- **Section label:** "WHAT YOU GET" — small caps, orange rule
- **Headline:** "Here's What's Inside Tradie Booster"
- **4 feature cards** (2x2 grid on desktop, stacked on mobile):
  - Black card, orange icon, white headline, charcoal body
  - 📹 Recorded Training Modules
  - 🎙️ Weekly Live Coaching & Q&A
  - 📋 Done-For-You Templates & Frameworks
  - 👥 Community of Trade Business Owners
- **Price callout box** below cards: Orange background, white text
  - "All of that for **$97/month**. No lock-in contracts."
- **CTA button** (orange, white text)

---

### 7. RESULTS SECTION
- **Background:** Black
- **Section label:** "REAL WORLD RESULTS" — small caps, orange rule
- **Headline:** White + orange mixed — "What This Actually Looks Like"
- **3 result cards** (horizontal on desktop):
  - Dark card `#1A1A1A`, orange accent border or top stripe
  - Perth Plumbers → $40K to $300K/month
  - Sydney Painter → $700K to $3M revenue
  - Sole Trader Electrician → Itemised pricing, dramatically improved margins
- **Subtext:** White italic — *"That's the floor. And the floor is still a meaningful win."*

---

### 8. OBJECTION SECTION
- **Background:** White, alternating with light grey `#F5F5F5`
- **Headline:** "You Might Be Thinking..."
- **2 objection blocks** (accordion or static):
  - "I can't afford $97/month" → one extra job pays for a full year
  - "I've tried coaches before" → they weren't trade coaches — this is different
- Orange accent bars / left borders on each block

---

### 9. WHO IT'S FOR SECTION
- **Background:** Black
- **2 columns:** ✅ This IS for you | ❌ This is NOT for you
- Orange ticks / red crosses
- White text on black

---

### 10. URGENCY + FINAL CTA SECTION
- **Background:** Orange (full bleed)
- **Headline:** White bold — "Every Month You Wait Is Another Month of Feast-or-Famine."
- **Body:** White — compounds, gets harder to unwind
- **Price:** Large white — "$97/month"
- **CTA button:** Black fill, white text (inverted for contrast on orange)
- **Guarantee text:** White — "30-day money-back. No questions asked."

---

### 11. FAQ SECTION
- **Background:** White
- **Section label:** "WE GET ASKED..." — matches Unorthodox Digital's style exactly
- Accordion-style Q&A, orange expand icons
- 6–7 questions (see landing page copy doc)

---

### 12. FOOTER
- Black background
- Vincere logo
- Contact: phone, email, website
- "© Vincere Coaching & Consulting"
- Privacy policy / terms links

## CTA Button Style (use consistently)

```
Background: #F47920
Text: #FFFFFF, Montserrat Bold, 16–18px
Border radius: 6–8px
Padding: 16px 32px
Hover: slightly darker orange #D4670F
```

---

## Mobile Notes
- Stack all 2-col sections vertically
- Keep orange/black contrast — do not lighten backgrounds on mobile
- Price badge: always visible, sticky bar option at bottom of screen worth testing
- CTA button: full width on mobile
