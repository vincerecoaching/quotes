Project Understanding

The Works,
Understood.

Our pricing is based on the supplied consultant documentation and Buildxact cost schedule. We have thoroughly reviewed the scope and are confident in our ability to deliver.

Scope Overview

The proposed works involve the demolition of the existing hardstand areas and construction of a new reinforced concrete parking slab, bored piers, civil transitions, drainage upgrades, landscaping and associated council interface works.

Particular care will be taken to maintain access, protect existing structures and manage stormwater flows adjacent to the existing car lift and pedestrian entry throughout all phases of the works.

Construction Sequence
  1. 1.Site establishment and protection
  2. 2.Service locating and surveying
  3. 3.Demolition and excavation
  4. 4.Drainage and subsoil works
  5. 5.Bored pier installation
  6. 6.Reinforced slab construction
  7. 7.Civil transitions and finishes
  8. 8.Landscaping and final handover
