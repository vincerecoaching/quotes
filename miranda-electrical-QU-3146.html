01

Lighting — Shed & Man Cave

Full LED lighting suite for the shed and man cave, designed to provide even illumination across all zones.

  • 8 × high bay LED lights — 5 switching groups
  • 8 × LED batten lights (shed, per layout)
  • 3 × LED batten lights over storage area
  • 2 × LED downlights (designated areas)
  • 1 × 100W LED floodlight — external/security
  • Power & lights supplied to man cave
02
🔌
Power Outlets

A comprehensive power point layout for general use, outdoor/damp locations, ceiling pendant supply and three-phase equipment.

  • 12 × internal double power points
  • 4 × weatherproof double power points
  • 2 × 15A single GPOs — ceiling pendant mount
  • 1 × 32A three-phase outlet
  • Dedicated 3-phase circuit from switchboard
03
⚡
Switchboard & Circuits

New shed switchboard installed complete, with all required circuits for the shed's power and lighting demands.

  • New shed switchboard — full supply & install
  • Main switch included
  • RCBO protection — individual per circuit
  • Circuit breakers sized to connected loads
  • All circuits clearly labelled
  • Dedicated circuit for hot water system
  • All power & lighting circuits to load requirements
04
📺
Additional Works — Man Cave

Extras included to complete the man cave setup.

  • Supply & install TV antenna for man cave
  • Power and lighting circuit to man cave

📋
Testing & Compliance

Full testing and certification of all works on completion to AS/NZS 3000 standards.

  • Test all circuits, outlets & lighting
  • Verify polarity, earth continuity & insulation resistance
