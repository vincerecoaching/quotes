160,000L Zincalume Tank Supply & Install

Supply and installation of a 160,000 litre Zincalume corrugated steel water tank — heavy duty, UV stabilised food-grade liner, engineered for large rural properties.

  • Rhino RT110 Zincalume corrugated steel
  • Food-grade poly liner included
  • 25-year manufacturer structural warranty
  • Leaf strainer & overflow outlet installed
  • Choice of 6 coloured liner options
02
🏗️
Tank Pad Preparation

Sand pad preparation using the site's existing sand. Structured to correctly bear the load of a 160,000L tank once filled.

  • Site assessment & level check
  • Excavation and compaction
  • Compacted blue metal base (100mm)
  • Suitable for tank load-bearing requirements

  • Site clean-up on completion
