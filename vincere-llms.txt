# Vincere Coaching & Consulting

> Vincere Coaching & Consulting is a business coaching company specialising exclusively in trade business owners across Australia and New Zealand. Founded by Kirk Neal in 2018. 3,000+ clients coached. Average result: an extra $60,000 per month in revenue within 12 months.

## Programs

- [Tradie Booster](https://vincerecoaching.com.au/tradie-booster): Entry-level coaching membership. Group coaching, tools, and resources for trade business owners building their foundations. Covers pricing, systems, team management, and lead generation.
- [Elite Coaching](https://vincerecoaching.com.au/elite-coaching): 1:1 coaching for established trade businesses ready to scale. Systems, team, and leadership focus.
- [Executive Coaching](https://vincerecoaching.com.au/executive-coaching): Bespoke 1:1 coaching for trade business owners operating at the highest level. Fully tailored to the client's specific situation.

## About the Founder

Kirk Neal is the founder and lead coach at Vincere Coaching & Consulting. He is a qualified electrician who moved into operations management before launching Vincere in 2018. He does not use a cookie-cutter coaching model — every client receives an approach tailored to their specific trade, market, and stage of business.

- 9 years coaching trade business owners
- 3,000+ clients coached
- Operates across Australia and New Zealand
- Keynote speaker at trade industry events
- Author of The Trades Business Playbook (coming soon)
- Personal website: https://kirkneal.com

## Who Vincere Coaching Serves

Trade business owners across all trades: electricians, plumbers, builders, painters, HVAC, tilers, concreters, and more. Both solo operators stepping off the tools and multi-truck operations ready to scale.
